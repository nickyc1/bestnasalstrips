# Breathe Right Review (2026)

Hands-on, bought at retail, not sponsored. Score 6.5/10.

Breathe Right Extra Strength is the original adhesive nasal strip and the category benchmark. It is single-use, drug-free, sold in nearly every drugstore at roughly $15 a box (about $0.50 a strip), and tested here as the baseline every other product is measured against.

## Scores (out of 10)
- Unboxing: 6.0, plain drugstore carton
- Setup: 8.5, peel, center, press; simplest of all
- Ease of use: 7.0, easy, but adhesion can lift with sweat or movement
- Pricing & value: 9.0, cheapest and most available option
- Customer support: 6.0, mass retail, no DTC support
- Effectiveness: 5.0, opens the nose, but did not fully stop snoring

## Verdict
Breathe Right is the cheapest, easiest way to test whether opening your nose helps at all. The lift is real but gentler than a magnetic dilator's, the adhesive lifted when our team moved, and like every nasal product tested it did not fully stop snoring, confirming a non-nasal cause. The right first test, not the final answer.
