# Best Nasal Strips for Snoring (2026)

For most people who snore through the nose, the **Intake Breathing** reusable dilator gives the strongest, most reliable opening night after night. **Breathe Right Extra Strength** is the best classic adhesive strip and the cheapest entry point. Athletes who sweat should choose **Dream Recovery Second Wind**. None of these treat obstructive sleep apnea.

## Award picks

- **Best overall:** Intake Breathing (92), strongest, most stable opening; reusable.
- **Best classic adhesive strip:** Breathe Right Extra Strength (85), proven, cheap, sold everywhere.
- **Best for athletes:** Dream Recovery Second Wind (83), band-style dilator, stronger open than a flat strip.
- **Best budget:** Massner (72), roughly 100 strips for about $21.
- **Best internal dilator:** Mute by Rhinomed (80), reusable, strong airflow at a low price.
