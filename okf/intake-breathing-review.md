# Intake Breathing Review (2026)

Hands-on, bought at retail, not sponsored. Score 6.3/10.

Intake is a magnetic external nasal dilator. The reusable band sits over the bridge of the nose and magnetic clips, attached with single-use adhesive tabs, tuck just inside each nostril. Starter kit is $39.95 (15-count, Black) and FSA/HSA eligible.

## Scores (out of 10)
- Unboxing: 8.0, premium, gadget-like packaging
- Setup: 6.5, adhesive tabs are fiddly; placement takes practice
- Ease of use: 6.0, comfortable once on, but a nightly tab ritual
- Pricing & value: 6.5, reusable band, consumable tabs; FSA helps
- Customer support: 7.0, easy Amazon returns
- Effectiveness: 5.0, strong nasal opening, but did not fully stop snoring

## Verdict
Intake opens the nose more than any adhesive strip the tester tried, and the "open" feeling is real. But the adhesive-tab setup is fiddly and it did not fully solve the tester's snoring, pointing to a non-nasal cause. Best for people whose snoring is purely from a narrow or congested nose.
